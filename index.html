About Me

Learning Designer.
Game Creator. Impact Maker.

I'm an L&D professional with 4+ years of experience designing gamified, AI-enabled, and experiential learning interventions for enterprise clients across India and North America.

Game Designs

Learning Experiences
Built Like Games

6 original game designs — from board games to card simulations — each built to turn behavior change into play.

Board Game
Mindset Movers
A Monopoly-inspired growth mindset board game where players earn Mindset Points by landing on Reflection, Challenge, and GRIT spaces. Players purchase Growth Assets and collaborate to upgrade them into Mastery Hubs.
Growth Mindset Self-Awareness Resilience
Card Game
Decide or Dare
Players draw cards and either "Dare" (share perspective) or "Decide" (choose best option from 4 responses). Peer dot-voting mechanism evaluates responses and declares a winner.
Negotiation Emotional Intelligence Customer Service
Board Game
Rise of the 4Cs
A Ludo-inspired team game where four teams navigate tokens through values-based challenge spaces, answering reflection questions tied to Curious, Courageous, Creative, and Caring behaviors.
Curious Courageous Creative Caring
Card Game
Tale of Cards
A reflective card activity using visual prompts to spark storytelling and meaningful discussion. Players share personal anecdotes, building empathy through open communication and peer recognition.
Reflection Empathy Social Learning
Card Game
UNO-Vation
UNO reimagined for professional development. Players respond to real workplace scenarios using Option Cards on a Scenario Board, balancing game mechanics with values-driven decision-making.
Collaboration Innovation Critical Thinking
Tabletop Simulation
The Verdict
A jury simulation set on fictional Paradise Island. Teams review sequentially released dossiers — crime records, whistleblower emails, testimonies — to determine guilt. No single correct verdict; quality of reasoning matters most.
Critical Thinking Bias Detection Evidence Evaluation
Flagship Programs

Learning Journeys
Built for Scale

Two signature programs designed for organizational-wide impact — blending AI, gamification, and behavioral design.

🌸
Give to Gain
IWD 2026 · 7-Day Digital Journey
A structured 7-day gamified experience for International Women's Day 2026, anchored around the belief that when we give intentionally, we gain collectively. Each day pairs a "Give" with a "Gain" — from Permission → Confidence to Strengths → Collective Power.
  • 10–15 minutes per day, fully self-paced
  • Delivered on Gametize platform
  • Inclusive design: women centred, everyone participates
  • Available as facilitator-led or fully asynchronous
  • 7 daily themes across equity, leadership & wellbeing
🤖
AIverse
Gamified AI Adoption Path · 4–6 Weeks
A 6-module asynchronous journey on Joyflar designed to drive behavioral AI adoption across organizations. Guided by virtual companion "Zoe," learners move from awareness to experimentation to recognition — without fear of getting it wrong.
  • 6 modules: Awareness → Curiosity → Playbook → Experiments → Peer Wins → Recognition
  • Designed for 4 learner personas across the AI adoption spectrum
  • Gamified missions, peer stories & "AI Innovator" leaderboard
  • Social layer with discussion threads and peer showcases
  • Weekly analytics dashboards for L&D teams
